## What you need 

- [Wallet](https://www.arconnect.io/download)
- `$AR`, you can get it from your favorite CEX

## Upload to ArDrive 

- Visit https://app.ardrive.io
- Login with ArConnect 
- Create a public Drive
- Then upload your file, you can upload image, video, text file, anything! ( Under 500Kb file size are FREE )
- Click your file and go to `details` section, then copy the `Data Tx ID`
<img width="100%" height="auto" src="/img/arnsde.png" type="image/png"></img>

## Buy your ArNS

#### To buy ArnS you need to have TestIO Token, here's how to request the token

- Go to Ar.io [Discord](https://discord.gg/c4rSSNV9v3)
    - In the `#arns-test` channel type `/apply`, answer all questions
    - <img width="100%" height="auto" src="/img/arnsap.png" type="image/png"></img>
    - For this question, answer with your Tx ID from previous Step
    - <img width="100%" height="auto" src="/img/arnsas.png" type="image/png"></img>

#### After receiving `Test IO`

- Go to https://arns.app
- Find your name  ( 8 digit++ )
- If it's available, click register now
- <img width="100%" height="auto" src="/img/arnsav.png" type="image/png"></img>
- Choose 1 Year
- Fill the `Arweave transaction ID ( Target ID )` with your Tx ID
- <img width="100%" height="auto" src="/img/arnsnt.png" type="image/png"></img>

And now you can access your ArNS with `https://your-arNS.ar-io.dev`.

Example https://megumii.ar-io.dev

#### `ar-io.dev` can be changed to any Gateways that listed here - > https://gateways.ar-io.dev
